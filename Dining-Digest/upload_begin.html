Step One: Upload your dining data

  1. Log-in to mycard.brown.edu.
  2. Select your meal plan ("20 Weekly Meals" or "Flex 460 Meals") under "Account".
  3. Select "View last 6 months" under "Statement Period.
  4. Click on the yellow "View Statement" button to retrieve your swipe history.
  5. Click on the grey "CSV" button in the top right corner of the page.
  6. Select the downloaded CSV in the upload section below.
